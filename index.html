How It Works

We Collect imagery with any drone and camera

Process and extract plot-level metrics

Visualize and analyze agricultural data

Download metrics to Excel or access via API

  1. Fly with Any Drone or Camera

    Collect imagery whenever you need it using any drone and camera. We support most sensors available on the market, including both RGB and multispectral — so whatever equipment you already own, it works with us.

  2. Process Into Precise Maps

    Your imagery is processed into accurate, georeferenced maps in as little as 30–60 minutes. No data science degree required. Upload, wait, and your field is ready to explore.

  3. Analyse With AI

    Our platform uses Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision to detect patterns across your fields that the human eye simply can't catch at scale — from plant stress and weed pressure to stand counts and crop vigour.

  4. Act on Your Data

    Download prescription maps, generate grower-ready reports, compare field performance over time, or push data directly into your existing workflow via API or CSV export. Insight without action is just a pretty picture — we make sure you get both.
